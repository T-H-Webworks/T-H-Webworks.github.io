FAQ

Common questions about our process and services.

How long does a typical project take?

+

Most projects take 6-12 weeks from start to finish. Basic websites typically take 6-8 weeks, while complex platforms can take 12-16 weeks.

What's your development process?

+

We follow a structured process: Discovery → Design → Development → Testing → Launch. You'll receive regular updates and previews throughout.

Do you offer ongoing support?

+

Yes, we offer various maintenance plans to keep your site secure and up-to-date. Check our Services page for detailed maintenance packages.

What's included in the price?

+

Our prices include design, development, testing, and launch. We also include 30 days of post-launch support to ensure everything runs smoothly.
